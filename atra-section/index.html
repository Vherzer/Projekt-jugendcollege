    About Us

    the update training GmbH is an educational institute with locations in Vienna, Carinthia and Styria. The spectrum of our offer ranges from specialist training, vocational orientation courses, to the placement of internships in different companies and sectors.   In our educational activities, we supervise, advise and assist unemployed people on their professional (re-) entry. We provide the specialist knowledge and social skills necessary to meet the changing demands of the job market. Our company is part of ÖSB Holding, which offers employment services in Austria in the areas of consulting, employment and qualification. Belonging to the group ensures a constant development and market stability of individual members. They can benefit from the synergy potential of the community by drawing on a wide range of best practices and learning from a variety of experiences, with the aim of meeting customers' needs and requirements in the best possible way.     About Us

    The vocational training center of the BFI Vienna The BAZ is the inter-company training center of the BFI Vienna for craft and technology with social commitment. The training at our locations in Vienna is of high quality, innovative and state of the art. With a total of 1,200 apprenticeship positions, we support young people and adults in gaining an apprenticeship in professions in metal technology, electrical engineering, wood technology and construction technology. Our close networking with industry enables our clients to complete company internships already during the apprenticeship as well as to successfully and sustainably integrate into the labor market after completing their training. Our well-trained graduates strengthen the economic region of Eastern Austria as specialists and thus contribute to economic growth in the region.
